Maruchan Ramen and Boiled Eggs

Description

This scrumptious delight of a meal found it's roots in China and now is shared worldwide as a great meal for anytime of day. The combonation of Flour, Seasoning Packet, and your favorite protein(mine happens to be eggs). Allow it to be a revered meal by all who get to experience it.

Ingredients

  • 3 Packets of your chosen variety of Maruchan Ramen.
  • 3 Large Eggs

Cooking Steps

    Heat the Stove Top:

  1. Once water is boiling, add egg's and allow to boil to preferred texture, i.e. runny or firm. Once done remove from stove top and place allow to cool down.

    2. Boil Noodles

  2. I like to break noodle bricks in half then add to boiling water, and allow to cook until my desired texture. Once done, drain pot to around 1/16th of water and mix in seasoning packet's and your choice of condiments.

    3. Mix and Enjoy

  3. After mixing condiments sufficiently, add the egg's and enjoy your meal.
