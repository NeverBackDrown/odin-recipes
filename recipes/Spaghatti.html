Spaghetti and HotDogs

Description

This scrumptious delight of a meal found it's roots in Siliy, Italy and now is shared worldwide as a great meal for anytime of day. The combonation of Flour, Cheese, Tomato Sauce, and let us not forget the delectable meat puree, Hot Dogs. Allow it to be a revered meal by all who get to experience it.

Ingredients

  • 1 Box of Your Favorite Pasta
  • 3/4 Cup Pizza Sauce
  • 2 morsels of Your Favorite HotDog brand, sliced to the width of your choosing.
  • 5 Ounces Mozzarella Cheese, Grated
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Ground Black Pepper
  • 3/2 Teaspoon Fresh Oregano, Optional
  • 2 Ounces of Butter
  • 1 Ounce of Salt

Cooking Steps

    Heat the Stove Top:

  1. Once water is boiling, add salt for taste. Then add spaghetti, add enough water to where there is an inch and a quarter or half of room left in pot. Let boil for 10-12 minutes for al dente(or reccommended on the box), then remove from stovetop and drain.

    2. Fry the Hotdogs

  2. Turn oven on low heat and add butter to pan. Once melted add sliced hotdogs and allow to sear to desired color and texture. Once there add pasta sauce and all other condiments, such as grated cheese, black pepper and oregano.

    3. Mix and Enjoy

  3. After mixing condiments and hotdog sufficiently, add to the spaghetti and continue mixing, until there are no more plain noodles, then serve and enjoy.
