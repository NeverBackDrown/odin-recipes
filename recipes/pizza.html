HomeMade Pepperoni Pizza

Description

This scrumptious delight of a meal found it's roots in Siliy, Italy and now is shared worldwide as a great meal for anytime of day. The combonation of Flour, Cheese, Tomato Sauce, and whatever toppings your mind can conceive. Allow it to be a revered meal by all who get to experience it.

Ingredients

  • 24 Ounces Pizza Dough
  • 3/4 Cup Pizza Sauce
  • 27 to 30 Slices of Pepperoni
  • 18 Ounces Mozzarella Cheese, Grated
  • 3/4 Teaspoon Ground Black Pepper
  • 3/2 Teaspoon Fresh Oregano, Optional
  • Flour for Rolling and Shaping Dough

Pizza Sauce Recipe

  • 3/4 Cup Fresh Tomato Sauce
  • 3/4 T easpoon Kosher Salt
  • 3/4 Teaspoon Ground Black Pepper
  • 3/4 Teaspoon Granulated Garlic
  • 3/4 Teaspoon Granulated Onion
  • 3/8 Teaspoon Red Pepper Flakes
  • 3/2 Teaspoon Olive Oil

Cooking Steps

    Preheat The Oven:

  1. Preheat Oven to 500Â°F. If using a pizza stone leave in oven for at least 20 minutes or until temperature is evenly distributed.

    2. Make The Sauce:

  2. Stir ingredients from previously mentioned sauce recipe, or your own.

    3. Roll Out The Dough

  3. Let dough get to room temperature, then roll out.

    4. Add The Toppings:

  4. Spread the pizza sauce on the dough, then add pepperoni. Top with the grated cheese than more pepperoni. To finish it off add some black pepper.

    5. Cook the Pizza:

  5. Add pizza to the oven. If using a Pizza Stone,After 6-8 minutes(depending on how toasted you like your meal) rotate to evenly distribute heat.// If using a skillet, leave in oven for 10 to 12 minutes, or until crust is desired color and texture.

    6. Slice and ENJOY:

  6. Let pizza rest for a minute or two. Then slice into as many pieces as desired, and enjoy.
